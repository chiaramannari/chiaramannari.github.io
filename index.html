Chiara Mannari

Graduate fellow at ISTI-CNR & Ph.D student in Artificial Intelligence

Chiara Mannari is Ph.D student in Artificial Intelligence at the University of Pisa and research fellow at WN lab, CNR-ISTI. Her background is in digital humanities. She got a bachelor’s and master’s degree in Digital Humanities at the University of Pisa and took part in several research projects in the field. Currently, she is taking part in HE2020 CODECS, an interdisciplinary research project to study the impact of digitalisation in agriculture. The aim of her research is to integrate a human centric AI vision with socio-technical process modelling and participatory methodologies to develop solutions helping to fulfill sustainable development goal and increasing trust in AI systems. Her research interests focus on human-centered AI, requirements engineering, end-user development, co-design and smart agriculture.
